I.
Standing

Eldest daughter of Melyra Galmir and Marcus Azane. Mother of Myrin. Partner — by her own arrangement and her own choosing — of Titano Rue.

In the framework Melyra has built around this branch — blood children first, marriage second — Myra is the one who has already done the work. The lineage obligation is complete. The arcane legacy is carried forward in her son. What she does next is hers.

She is the senior daughter of the Galmir-Azane line, the one her younger sisters look to in different ways for different reasons. Her composure is the household's steady thread.

III.
The Blood Child Fulfilled

Myrin Galmir was born of Myra and Titano Rue roughly four to five months before the series opens. He is a Blood Child in the strict canonical sense — the arrangement Melyra prizes above all formal marriage in this branch — and his Apex status was confirmed in infancy.

His last name is Galmir. The naming follows the alternating convention with Galmir on this side of the alternation; the more powerful line takes the name when there is one, and the Galmir name on a Novan-track infant carries weight Melyra is not interested in giving away. Melyra and Myra both offered the Rue name to Titano, breaking convention deliberately, to give him an heir for a house that has no one else left. Titano declined, with the same quiet finality he carries everything else.

The decline did not damage the partnership. If anything, it clarified what it was.

✦ ✦ ✦
IV.
What Comes Next

The lineage obligation is closed. Myra is, in Melyra's framework, free.

What that freedom looks like, day to day, is largely an open question at series start. Her arrangement with Titano holds. Her son is at Aurora under her mother's roof and her sisters' hands. Her position in the household's political life — quiet, central, deferred to without ceremony — is not something she has indicated any interest in trading for anything else.

She is the daughter Melyra does not have to think about. That is its own kind of standing.

VI.
Relations
Melyra Galmir
Mother · Matriarch
Eldest daughter of the matriarch. Inheritor of Melyra's framework — and the first of her sisters to satisfy it. The relationship is uncomplicated by the standards of this house: respect, alignment, no friction worth naming.
Titano Rue
Partner · Father of Myrin
Warmaster of the War Force. Last living member of House Rue. Father of her son. The arrangement is by mutual choice and survives at its own quiet pace. His decline of the Rue name for Myrin was received without conflict — she had offered it, with Melyra, knowing how it might land. It landed exactly as it landed.
Myrin Galmir
Son · Blood Child · Apex Infant
Roughly four to five months old at series start. Confirmed Apex. Present at Aurora under his grandmother's roof and his aunts' hands. The center of his mother's quieter life.
Diamyra Azane
Younger Sister
The warrior sister. The one absent from the opening arc on account of an unresolved tournament rivalry with Dixie Vendrith. Myra watches her sister move through the world with the older-sister composure of someone who has already chosen her own path and trusts Diamyra to find hers.
Myrella Galmir
Younger Sister · The Blood Mother
The center of the family's most consequential arrangement. Where Myra's blood-child obligation closed quietly with Titano, Myrella's opens publicly, with the Crown. The two sisters share the framework but not the scale.
Maylyra Azane
Younger Sister
Currently being prospected for. Myra is the example the prospecting refers to, whether anyone says so or not.
