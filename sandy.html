I.
Standing

Fifth of Maia's daughters. Betrothed to Koran Navori — Khlora Rhill's youngest, approximately 15 at series start, born around the same time as Lessio Locsird.

Of all Maia's placements, this one carries the lightest immediate weight on the daughter. The age gap is 22 years. The marriage is confirmed but deferred until Koran is older. Sandy naturally dominates the dynamic by temperament and age alone, with nothing forced or negotiated about it. She lives, on the ground, with fewer obligations and a longer horizon than any of her older sisters.

III.
The Rhill Placement

The match is structurally quiet and strategically sharp. Sandy marries into Navori blood — not the Rhill line directly, but the family of Khlora Rhill's husband Roven, on Khlora's ancestral side. That means Maia's influence lands adjacent to a council voting seat, not through the primary line. Adjacency to a seat is not the same as holding one, but it is far more valuable than a Crown-adjacent administrative post in Martinhal would have been.

Koran is the youngest of a family with a long history. He stands alone in his generational group — separated from his nearest sibling by nearly 60 years. He was born around the same time as Lessio, part of a world-wide quiet instinct: many families chose to bring new life into the world when the Rebirth arrived, as a form of rejoicing and honoring the cosmic event.

The narrative contrast is deliberate. Koran is 15 with a deferred marriage and an entire childhood still ahead of him. Lessio is 16 with a council-locked timeline and a compressed obligation schedule that begins before his eighteenth birthday. Two boys of the same generation, two entirely different weights — and Sandy sits on the quieter side.

Maia Galmir
Mother · The Architect
Placed her into the deferred Rhill match with a 22-year age gap — a setup that Sandy will grow comfortably into on her own timeline. Maia knows exactly what she has given her daughter here. Sandy knows Maia knows.
Koran Navori
Betrothed · House Navori, Province of Rhill
Approximately 15 at series start. Born around the time of the Rebirth. The youngest of Khlora and Roven's children, separated from his nearest sibling by nearly 60 years. He carries the Navori name (8th-born in the family's naming register).
Rhyca Locsird
Geographic Neighbor · Existing Friction
Placed in Martinhal by the Valdren betrothal. Sandy and Rhyca operate in the same broader region — same continent, different provinces — with pre-existing friction that never quite resolves.
Margaret Vendrith
Sister · Adjacent Thread
The two middle daughters — Margaret in Martinhal proper via Valdren, Sandy in Rhill via Navori. Their threads run parallel without overlapping.
