I.
Personality

Warm. Quietly sharp. Emotionally perceptive. She has carried her trauma privately for fourteen years and does not feel entitled to it — operates under the belief that everyone lost something that day and she has no right to make hers bigger than anyone else's. This is not weakness. It is a misplaced sense of fairness that has become habit.

She shares much of Rhyca's demeanor — warm, patient, finds humor naturally. They look the most alike of all the siblings, and the resemblance is more than physical. She minimizes her own experience habitually. Her wounds are managed, not processed. She has probably never said out loud how much she needed this drive to Aurora.

II.
The Day of the Abduction
Age 6 · Fourteen years ago

She was napping in the same room as infant Lessio when Sapphire entered. She woke to the sounds of Sapphire and Emerald clashing. She hid under the bed — terrified, too small and too young to do anything else. She has repressed the visual memory of Sapphire and actively steers her thoughts away from it.

When the noise stopped she crawled out. She was found clinging to Emerald's body, crying. She was the last person to hold Emerald. Then Sonya-Lee found her — and Aurea witnessed her mother completely uncontained, grief and rage simultaneously, before Jonzhento arrived. A six-year-old who had just come out of hiding, watching the most feared woman in the world break open in front of her. It compounded her trauma in its own distinct way.

At six her memory is more conscious and coherent than infant memory — she remembers sounds, cold, what she saw, her mother's face. Real childhood memory she has spent fourteen years actively managing. This experience has never been fully spoken aloud to anyone. Everyone around her likely underestimated what she absorbed because she was so young.

IV.
Relations
Lessio Locsird
Brother · Future Peer Dynamic
They are close enough in age to operate as peers rather than older/younger siblings — four years apart. This will define their bond as distinct from every other sibling relationship Lessio has. He will not feel handled by her. She will not mother him. They will be the only two who meet each other without the asymmetry.
Rhyca Locsird
Elder Sister · Second Mother
Rhyca took her under her wing early. Functions as second mother and elder sister simultaneously. They look the most alike of all the siblings — the physical resemblance mirrors the temperamental one. Their closeness feels written into them. In the convoy, Aurea sits nearest to her.
Samantha-Lilly Toraiyo
Sister · Sam's Soft Spot
Sam holds a quiet soft spot for Aurea — expressed only through restraint, never warmth. Sam rarely brings her full force to bear on Aurea. Aurea is one of the only people who can plead with Sam to stop — and sometimes Sam will. This dynamic lives entirely in what Sam chooses not to do.
Sonya-Lee Toraiyo
Mother · The Memory of Uncontainment
Sonya-Lee found her first — found a six-year-old clinging to Emerald's body. Aurea witnessed her mother completely uncontained before Jonzhento arrived. The image of Sonya-Lee in that state, at that moment, seen at six years old, is part of what she has spent fourteen years managing.
