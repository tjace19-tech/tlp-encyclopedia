I.
Standing

Son of Maini Galmir and Lyle Helsird. Grandson of Maia Galmir. Carries the Helsird surname per the alternating convention running through his father's house.

Doxin is the only male in his generation on Maia's entire branch — among seven Maia daughters, two grandchildren, and one consort, he is alone in his age group and his gender on this line. The structural fact is notable but not yet narratively activated. At series start, he is observed alongside the other youngest members of the household; he does not yet drive scenes.

His canon beyond lineage is open. Like Daisy, the material has been deliberately left room to develop.
