I.
The Branch
Eldest legitimate. Sole Head of House.

There are two Galmir branches. This is the one the law recognizes.

Maia Galmir was born of a formal political marriage — the legitimate firstborn of House Galmir's main line, and by strict inheritance tradition, the eldest lawful heir. She is two centuries younger than her half-sister Melyra, but birth order is not the question at Lake Port. Legitimacy is. Maia holds the seat, the keep, and the sole designation of Head of House. The Azane branch, by its own choice, left.

Where Melyra's household orbits the Blood of Myrient and the obligations that arcane legacy imposes, Maia's household orbits consolidated political reach. Every daughter has a defined strategic role. Every marriage has a negotiated condition. The web Maia has built across Gemlyria is the single largest political structure in the kingdom not housed under the Crown.

This is the branch without the wings. It was never supposed to need them.

II.
The Architecture of Alliance
Maia's marriage map, generation in motion.

Every one of Maia's daughters carries a strategic designation. Claudia extends the branch's reach into Astravar by way of House Solarn — a deliberate and provocative thread across the continent. Margaret and Sandy hold two distinct threads into Martinhal, one of old money and combat tradition, the other of Crown-adjacent political influence. Jackie is the Martial thread, negotiated against House Tyrana on terms that Tyrana accepted publicly and filed privately. Holly was secured early to Lessio Locsird — a betrothal arranged before cosmology made it historic.

This is the deliberate structural opposite of the Azane branch. Where Melyra's daughters move at their own pace — arcane obligation first, marriage second — Maia's daughters move to a schedule. The two matriarchs have never tried to harmonize the two systems. They are not meant to harmonize.

The Blood of Myrient is absent from this branch entirely. Holly's child, when it comes, will be born a Locsird by Royal House rule — the first-born child of the Rebirth, carrying the other House's name. Maia has noted this. Maia takes a quiet, unadvertised delight in the fact that her grandchild through Holly will carry the Galmir name in the second slot of the alternating convention, while the cosmically-significant one is not hers. These things balance in her ledger without being spoken aloud.
