I.
Standing

Youngest daughter of Melyra Galmir and Marcus Azane. The only one of her sisters Melyra has not yet moved on at all.

In Melyra's framework, Mimyr sits in a category of her own at series start: not yet a priority. No blood-child negotiation active. No marriage arrangement under consideration. No candidate prospecting. Her mother has simply not initiated anything for her, and the household has taken the lack of initiation as the point.

She is the youngest. There is time. By the household's standards there is a great deal of time.

✦ ✦ ✦
II.
Where She Is

Mimyr lives at Aurora at series start, inside the Galmir-Azane orbit, inside the standing of being the matriarch's youngest daughter. She is present in evening settings, present in family gatherings, present in the kind of social calendar a daughter of this house carries by default.

What she does with that standing — what she becomes interested in, what she makes a point of, what she eventually wants — is, at series open, a question the canon holds open on purpose.
